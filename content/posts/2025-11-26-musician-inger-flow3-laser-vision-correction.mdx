## When the familiar becomes uncomfortable

KSA Silmakeskus was already on INGER's radar before she ever walked through the door.

"I'd heard a lot about KSA — from friends and online. I knew they had a talented team and did procedures reliably. Several people close to me had already been through it, and KSA's name kept coming up."

Prescription glasses had been part of INGER's life since primary school. Contact lenses arrived in secondary school. At first, they felt like freedom — no more glasses. Then came the reckoning: this couldn't go on.
