## We've opened a new clinic in Tartu

Last October, we announced something big: KSA relocated our Tartu clinic, and we marked the occasion with a proper opening celebration.

Finding the right space in the heart of Tartu took time and careful thought, but we're confident the new location in Kvartal meets expectations—both ours and our patients'. The interior design firm Lävi created a welcoming, beautiful space that sets the tone for everything we do.

Opening week brought partners, new patients, and familiar faces through the doors. Dr. Ants Haavel, our founder and lead ophthalmologist, spoke about [laser procedures and vision correction](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/). We heard real stories from patients Kristina and Johannes Sikk, watched their testimonial videos, and had genuine conversations about their experience. Visitors got to explore the new facilities and have their vision screened for myopia.

We're genuinely grateful to everyone who came to visit us during opening week and celebrate this milestone with us.

## What we offer at KSA Silmakeskus Tartu

* Pre-procedure assessment for [Flow3 laser correction](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) – for those wearing glasses or contact lenses for myopia
* Comprehensive vision audit for adults
* Vision screening for children
