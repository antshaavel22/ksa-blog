## Looking Back at 2022 at KSA Silmakeskus

As another year draws to a close, we do what we always do: pause, reflect, and look back on what we're grateful for. At KSA Silmakeskus, 2022 was exceptional in almost every way.

Here's what made this year special.

### Nearly 2,000 Patients Chose Better Vision

Our deepest gratitude goes to our patients. **Almost 2,000 people decided 2022 was the year to say goodbye to glasses and contact lenses.** They chose KSA to transform their vision — and their lives.

These aren't just numbers to us. Each procedure represents someone who woke up the next morning seeing clearly, naturally, for the first time in decades. That matters.

### More Capacity, More Care

We expanded our clinic facilities in 2021, and 2022 proved the value of that investment. We welcomed more patients for vision screening, eye health audits, and children's eye exams. **Nearly 1,000 young patients came through our doors for sight checks** — because good vision starts early.

### The Latest Laser Technology Arrived

In early 2022, we upgraded to the newest Schwind Amaris laser system. We replaced our proven Amaris 1050RS with the latest model in the series — **the only one of its kind in Estonia.** This technology means safer procedures, more precision, and better outcomes for our patients.

This is what modern [laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) looks like.

### We Returned to Europe's Biggest Eye Surgery Conference

For years, the pandemic kept us home. But in September 2022, the KSA team traveled to Milan for the **40th European Refractive Surgery Congress (ESCRS)** — one of Europe's largest annual gatherings, with over 7,500 attendees from 130 countries.

We refreshed our knowledge, connected with colleagues worldwide, and brought the latest thinking back to Tallinn. Sustainability in eye care was a major theme — something we're deeply committed to.

### In-Person Patient Days Made a Comeback

For two years, patient information days happened online only. In 2022, we held them **in person again** — and the response was overwhelming. People want to sit in our clinic, ask questions face-to-face, and meet the team.

We'll continue hosting patient days in 2023. Our patients tell us it's the best way to get real answers about [Flow3](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) and the entire journey ahead.

### Notable Patients, Inspired Stories

We love meeting interesting people with inspiring stories. In 2022, several well-known names chose to correct their vision with us — including **actress Adeele Jaago and singer Märten Männiste.**

But the real stars are the thousands of everyday patients whose lives changed because they trusted us with their sight.
