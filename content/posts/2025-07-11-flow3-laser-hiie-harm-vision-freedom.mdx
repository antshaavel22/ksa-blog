Yesterday, Hiie Härm walked out of KSA Silmakeskus glasses-free—and beaming. She brought so much warmth and optimism into the clinic that morning, you'd have thought she was already celebrating. But the real celebration came after. For 18 years, Hiie had worn glasses. Not happily. Not even after nearly two decades of adjustment.

Then she chose [Flow3 laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/).

## What Made You Decide to Break Free From Glasses?

**Hiie:** "Both my sister and many of my friends have had their vision corrected at KSA. They were so happy with their results, and they spoke so warmly about their experience. That, combined with my own desire for excellent vision, made the choice clear. I had to try it."

## How Do You Think Your Life Will Change Now?

**Hiie:** "Honestly? I already feel more confident, more myself. I can't wait for summer—I want to wear normal sunglasses again. And I'm looking forward to going to balls with my husband without worrying about my glasses slipping or getting in the way while we dance."

## Why Flow3?

Hiie's story is far from unique at KSA. Since Dr. Ants Haavel founded the clinic over 20 years ago, we've completed more than 55,000 procedures. But what matters most? Our own clinical team chose [Flow3 for their own eyes](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/). When the surgeons who perform the procedure every day choose it for themselves and their families, that says everything.

Flow3 is flapless surface laser technology. No cutting, no flap. That means faster healing (around one week), better stability for active lifestyles, and a procedure that's gentler on the cornea.

If you've spent years—or decades—wondering what life would feel like without glasses or contact lenses, Hiie's answer might resonate: it feels like freedom.
