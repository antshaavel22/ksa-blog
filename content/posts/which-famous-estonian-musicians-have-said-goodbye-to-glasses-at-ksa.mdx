**If KSA Vision Clinic had its very own Walk of Fame, the stars would include many of Estonia's most loved actors, singers, athletes, and public figures who have entrusted their eyesight to us. In this article, we're highlighting musicians who now enjoy super vision thanks to KSA!**

Why do so many artists choose **laser vision correction**? For performers, good eyesight is crucial. Dimly lit stages, tiny sheet music notes, following the right camera cue, or glasses slipping at the wrong moment—shortsightedness can really test a performer's confidence.

## Stage Confidence Without Glasses

Singer and musician **Laura Põldvere** had her vision corrected at KSA nearly ten years ago. She recalls a stressful concert before her procedure: "During the Estonian Music Days, we had an important live broadcast on Klassikaraadio. Someone at the back of the hall was supposed to give us the start signal—but I couldn't see it! I was terrified I'd start singing too early. In the end, they had to wave a big white sheet of paper at me so I could notice. Even then, I wasn't 100% sure."

Since her laser eye surgery, such worries are long gone. Laura says the world looks even more beautiful than before.

Professional keyboard player **Rene Puura**, known from Karl-Erik Taukar's band, Terminaator, and Uku Suviste's group, has enjoyed perfect vision for nearly four years thanks to the **Flow³ procedure**. He remembers how, during energetic live shows, his glasses would sometimes fall off mid-performance, forcing a bandmate to pause and hand them back.

Today, he says his new vision feels like a mix of 4K and 3D cinema: "Sometimes I catch myself just staring at something, amazed at how well I can see!"

Singer **Artjom Savitski** often relied on sheet music during performances, which meant carrying his glasses everywhere. Forgetting them caused stress and frustration. Since his procedure, that stress is gone: "Now I can actually see the audience and their emotions—it makes me ten times happier as an artist!"

## Everyday Life Without Contact Lenses

Beyond the professional advantages that come with excellent vision, many musicians who have undergone laser eye surgery at KSA also share how much it has improved their everyday quality of life.

Beloved singer **Eda-Ines Etti** had her eyes corrected at KSA almost 16 years ago. A long-time contact lens user, she found lenses especially bothersome in summer—when she wanted to swim or enjoy spontaneous outdoor activities. Today, she says her travels and summers are far more enjoyable thanks to clear vision.

Singer-songwriter **Kadri Voorand** underwent the Flow3 procedure over five years ago. She loves to travel, hike, and explore alone. Before surgery, she often worried about losing a lens and being unable to see properly without help.

After her procedure, that anxiety disappeared: "Now I can even go surfing alone without worrying I won't see the shore when I come out of the water. Life is so much easier—and I wish I'd done it ten years earlier!"

## A Life-Changing Decision

One of Estonia's most popular singers and winner of multiple [Estonian Music Awards](https://www.muusikaauhinnad.eu/), **Stefan**, had dreamed of clear vision since childhood. Strong **myopia** made him feel insecure, clumsy, and uncomfortable.

After his Flow3 procedure at **KSA Silmakeskus**, he describes his new vision as life-changing: "Now I can look my girlfriend in the eyes and tell her she's the most beautiful girl in the world—and actually see it clearly."

Whether you're just starting your career, performing on the biggest stages, or making music as a hobby—clear vision makes life easier, freer, and more beautiful.**
