## How Flow3 Works: No Cuts, No Flaps, Just Results

[Flow3](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) is a flapless, touchless laser procedure. The laser gently reshapes only the outer surface of your cornea — no surgical cuts, no corneal flap. This is fundamentally different from older LASIK techniques, and it matters.

During the procedure, the laser removes only the corneal epithelium (the outermost layer), which is one of the body's fastest-healing tissues. It regrows naturally within three days.

Once healed, your eye looks and performs exactly like a natural eye under the microscope. No scar tissue. No weakness. Your cornea tolerates rubbing, contact, and impact just as it did before surgery.

This is why athletes, divers, martial artists, and skydivers choose Flow3. After just one week of recovery, you can return to full activity — contact sports, water, everything.

## Speed That Matters

The Schwind Amaris 1050RS works with remarkable speed. For myopia correction, the laser typically treats one eye in just 15 seconds (exact time depends on prescription strength). Both eyes together? About 30 seconds.

That speed isn't a marketing gimmick. It means less eye fatigue during the procedure, less stress for the patient, and optimal safety. Shorter laser time = greater precision and comfort.
