We opened a new clinic in Tartu!

**At the end of October, we shared exciting news — KSA Tartu clinic changed its location, and a festive opening ceremony took place to mark the event.**

Finding a new space in the heart of Tartu, in the **Kvartal centre**, was a long and careful process, but we are confident that it meets the expectations of both our team and our clients. The cosy and beautiful interior was created by the interior architecture studio **Lävi**.

## Grand Opening Celebration at KSA Tartu

During the opening event, our new premises were filled with wonderful cooperation partners as well as both new and returning clients. Guests had the opportunity to listen to a presentation by **KSA ophthalmologist Dr. Ants Haavel** about laser procedures and to hear the story of our clients — siblings Kristina and Johannes Sikk. Together, we watched videos made with their participation and discussed interesting questions. Visitors also had a chance to explore the new rooms and check their vision for nearsightedness.

We sincerely thank everyone who came during the opening week and joined us to get acquainted with our new clinic!

Photos: Aive Urberg

## Services Offered at KSA Eye Centre Tartu

*   **Pre-procedure Flow3 eye examination** for people who wear glasses or contact lenses
*   Comprehensive eye examination Audit for adults
*   Eye examinations for children

## How to Find Us in Kvartal Centre

The new clinic is located on the 2nd floor of the Kvartal centre. You can reach it:

*   From Riia Street, via entrance 2 (stairs or elevator)
*   From parking levels P1 and P2, via entrance 2 (stairs or elevator)

**Opening hours:** Mon–Thu 9:00–17:00
