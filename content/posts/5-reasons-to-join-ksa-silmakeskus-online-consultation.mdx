**5 reasons to join KSA Silmakeskus online consultation**

**Tänapäeva kiire elutempo juures otsime aina enam lahendusi, mis võimaldavad meil mugavalt ja tõhusalt oma tervise eest hoolt kanda. KSA Silmakeskus, juhtiv lasersilmakliinik Eestis, on astunud sammu tulevikku, pakkudes nüüd online\-konsultatsioone nägemistervise ja silmade laseroperatsioonide teemal. Miks tasuks kaaluda just online\-konsultatsiooni teenust? Toome välja viis peamist põhjust.**

### 1\. The best choice for people with nearsightedness

We know from experience that wearing glasses or contact lenses can often be quite a challenge. According to a KSA Silmakeskus survey among 1,500 former glasses wearers, as many as 76% said that glasses and contacts caused discomfort — from eye strain, redness, and dryness to headaches. Wouldn’t it be great to quickly get reliable advice or someone to help you understand how to improve your situation? That’s exactly what KSA Silmakeskus’ online consultation offers — the best opportunity for people aged 18–45 with up to -9 diopters to get clear, professional guidance without leaving home.

### Fast and convenient

An online consultation allows you to get answers to all your questions from anywhere – even without leaving your home. There’s no need to plan a visit, take time off work, travel, or sit in traffic – everything happens virtually. All you need is a computer or smart device with an internet connection, and soon a friendly KSA Silmakeskus specialist will greet you on the other side of the screen. After just 20 minutes of a highly useful session, you’ll have all the answers you need to take your next informed step.

### 3\. High-quality and personalized advice

At KSA Silmakeskus, online consultations are conducted by top specialists in their field with the same dedication as in-person clinic visits. During the consultation, you’ll receive personalized answers to your questions about your vision, detailed information about laser eye procedures, and an explanation of how the process works. You’ll also get an expert assessment of whether you may be a suitable candidate for laser correction. If you wish, the specialist will help you choose a convenient time for a full eye examination at the clinic.

### 4\. The online consultation is FREE

Yes, you read that right! At the moment, it’s possible to get professional advice from our eye clinic specialists completely free of charge. In times when life is getting more expensive and the new year brings tax increases, it’s wise to take advantage of opportunities that don’t strain your wallet.

### 5\. The first step toward clearer vision

An online consultation is a great way to take your first step toward sharper vision. After the session, you’ll receive a clear overview of your options for improving your eyesight. If you decide to proceed with a laser eye procedure, the next steps will already be simpler and smoother.

How to get started?

Registering for a KSA Silmakeskus online consultation is quick and easy. Visit the KSA website, choose a convenient time, and book your appointment. It might just be that this virtual meeting marks the beginning of your beautiful journey toward a glasses-free life. Book your time today!
