## 10 Reasons Laser Eye Surgery Might Be Right for You

Have you been considering laser eye correction to ditch your glasses and contact lenses? As Estonia's leading laser eye clinic, KSA Silmakeskus has performed over 55,000 procedures. We've seen firsthand why so many people with myopia choose [laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) instead. Here are 10 reasons that might resonate with you.

## 1. You're Active, and Glasses Get in the Way

Contact sports — boxing, martial arts, surfing — and glasses don't mix well. Neither does motorsport. After laser surgery, you won't deal with frozen lenses on the ski slope, a contact lens falling out mid-volleyball, or awkward moments in the changing room. The [Flow3 procedure](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) is especially popular with athletes because it's flapless and safer for active lifestyles.

## 2. Your Eyes No Longer Tolerate Contact Lenses

You've managed for years, but now your eyes are rebelling: dry, red, uncomfortable. Most people can wear contacts for around 15 years before discomfort sets in. Ignoring the problem invites infections and long-term damage. Laser correction eliminates this altogether.

## 3. Your Vision Isn't Sharp Enough in Certain Situations

Struggling to see clearly while driving? Night vision not what it should be? These are common concerns that [laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) often resolves. It's worth booking a consultation to discuss your specific situation with our specialists.

## 4. You Get Headaches from Eye Strain

Glasses that are the wrong prescription force your eyes to work harder than they should. Frames pressing on your nose and temples add to the strain. The result: regular headaches. Many patients find relief through laser vision correction.

## 5. You Want One Less Thing to Worry About

Laser eye surgery gives you back time and mental space. No more buying, losing, or replacing glasses. No more daily contact lens maintenance and costs. That's freedom most people don't expect until they have it.

## 6. You Prefer How You Look Without Glasses

Some people love glasses as a style statement. Others don't. If you're in the second camp, glasses can affect your confidence. Many patients report feeling like they've gained a new appearance after laser surgery — and a significant confidence boost comes with it.

## 7. Your Vision Has Stabilised

Laser procedures work best when your vision is stable. For long-lasting results, we recommend the procedure for people whose prescription hasn't changed significantly for at least two years. If you're in this position, now might be the right time.

## 8. You're Tired of the Ongoing Cost

Glasses, replacement lenses, coatings, contact lens solutions, new contacts every year — it adds up. A one-time laser procedure often costs less over your lifetime than a decade of vision aids.

## 9. You Want a Quick Recovery

Flow3 is flapless laser surgery. No corneal flap means faster healing — typically around one week for most activities. You're back to normal life quickly, without the extended downtime of older laser techniques.

## 10. Your Eye Care Team Chose It for Themselves

The strongest endorsement? Our entire clinical team chose [Flow3](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) for their own vision correction. When the specialists who perform the procedure choose it for themselves, that tells you something.

## Is Laser Surgery Right for You?

Not everyone is a candidate for laser eye surgery. Some patients are better suited to [ICB lens replacement](https://ksa.ee/en/icb-time), which works differently and has its own advantages.
