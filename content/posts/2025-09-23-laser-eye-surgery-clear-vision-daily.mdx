Wearing glasses while active is frustrating. Contacts shift. Sweat happens. ## Work and Daily Focus

Desk work, screen time, detailed tasks—when your vision isn't optimal, fatigue sets in faster. Your eyes work harder. Your concentration drifts. Corrected vision changes that. Your workday becomes easier. You see your screen sharply. You strain less. You focus longer. You also stop cleaning smudged lenses or dealing with dry contact discomfort by day's end. That's a small thing until it's your daily reality—then it's everything.
