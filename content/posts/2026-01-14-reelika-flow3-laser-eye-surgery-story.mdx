## From Foggy Glasses to Clear Vision

Midway through summer, Reelika Tammeoru walked out of KSA Silmakeskus without glasses for the first time in years. Her reason for choosing [Flow3 laser surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) was simple and practical: she wanted to drive comfortably. But the benefits? Those turned out to be much broader.

Reelika had spent nearly 13 years in the UK—what started as a short trip became a London-based life. She studied marketing, built a seven-year career in her field, and eventually moved back to Estonia with her partner. That's when the vision problem became impossible to ignore.

## Wet Lenses and Driving Frustration

Reelika had never liked wearing glasses. She'd spent most of her adult life squinting through life, especially on holiday. But when she returned to Estonia, bought a car, and needed a driving licence, her glasses became a genuine problem.

Driving with glasses meant constant fogging—especially once face masks became part of daily life. The lenses would mist up in the rain, fog over in cold weather, and create one more thing to worry about on the road. But there was something deeper, too.

Reelika loves nature. She loves Estonian nature specifically, and she found herself frustrated that wet lenses kept getting in the way of those moments. Hiking in the rain, driving past autumn forests, watching the seasons change—simple pleasures complicated by the simple fact of needing glasses.

She'd visited KSA for an initial consultation months earlier, but hesitated. She rescheduled. Then rescheduled again. It wasn't until about a month before her driving test that she finally decided: enough. Time to just do it.

## Dr. Haavel's Calm Presence

On procedure day, Reelika felt the usual pre-surgery nerves. When she arrived at the clinic and chose her protective sunglasses, the anxiety kicked in. Then Dr. Ants Haavel stepped in—and the conversation shifted entirely.

"He got my mind off the procedure," she explains. "I even learned that my eye pigment is beautiful." By the time it was over, Reelika realised the experience hadn't been difficult at all. The protective lens period and recovery were straightforward. She credits Dr. Haavel's professionalism and 20+ years of experience for making it feel effortless.

## The Bonus Benefits

One month after surgery, Reelika passed her driving test. She now holds a valid UK driving licence, and by her own assessment, her vision is "five stars."

But the real wins go deeper. Her partner enjoys simply looking at her eyes. She drives with genuine pleasure now—no fog, no frustration, just the road and the view. And she's never buying expensive glasses again.

When you remove friction from daily life, it ripples outward. For Reelika, [Flow3 surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) wasn't just about passing a test. It was about reclaiming the small moments: the Estonian forest in winter, the drive home at sunset, the simple freedom of seeing clearly without anything in the way.

That's worth more than glasses ever could be.
