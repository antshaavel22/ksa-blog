Did you know that sports injuries are one of the most common causes of blindness in children? **90% of these injuries** can easily be prevented with proper eye protection.

For the same reason that professional athletes wear protective equipment such as helmets, children need **protective glasses** to protect their eyes – even if they do not need corrective lenses! Sport-specific protective glasses can have prescription or non-prescription lenses, and their features include: lightweight **impact-resistant lens materials**, polarisation, UV protection, a wraparound style, and rubber padding. We offer modern frames in a variety of fun colours to keep your young athlete's vision safe while staying stylish.
