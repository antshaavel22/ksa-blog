## Quick relief for tired eyes

There's a simple technique our eye specialists recommend to ease discomfort—and it takes just five minutes.

**The four-step process:**

1. Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water.
2. Dampen a clean cloth under warm (not hot) running water.
3. Close your eyes and place the cloth over your eyelids for five minutes.
4. Repeat as needed throughout the day.

## Why this works

Eye discomfort can stem from many causes—screen time, dry air, fatigue, or simple strain. Warmth helps relax the eye muscles and encourages natural tear production, offering quick relief.

The key is using a fresh, clean cloth each time and applying the compress several times daily if needed. Consistency matters more than intensity.
