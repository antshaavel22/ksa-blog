## Recovery Essentials: What to Expect

After your [Flow3 laser procedure](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/), plan for roughly one week of recovery. During this time, rest is non-negotiable. Let your eyes be. Avoid unnecessary strain or stressful work that demands focus.

The first two to three days are typically the most noticeable—you may experience mild discomfort, grittiness, or tearing. Beyond that, recovery is individual. Most patients return to normal life within about seven days. We've seen firsthand, and research confirms it: a positive mindset, nourishing whole-food nutrition, and a stress-free environment accelerate healing significantly. Of course, follow your surgeon's prescribed eye drop routine precisely—these drops are essential to the entire recovery process.

So what do you do with all this unexpected free time? Here's the key: minimize screen time completely. No scrolling through emails or social media. Instead, use this week intentionally. We've put together seven ideas to help you rest, grow, and make the most of your healing time.

## 1. Sleep Without Guilt

Give yourself permission to lie in bed guilt-free. Doze. Dream. Sleep without checking the clock. Catch up on every hour of lost sleep. Your body heals faster when it's well-rested—it's not laziness, it's medicine.

## 2. Try Meditation

If you've ever wondered how meditation works, recovery week is the perfect time to find out. Start with just a few minutes. Turn your attention inward. Let thoughts pass through without judgment. Discover the peace of genuine quiet.

## 3. Walk in Nature

Dress for the weather, bring a friend (or their dog), and step outside. Choose a forest, a park, or the seaside—or better yet, visit somewhere new each day. Solo one day, with a friend the next, with a dog the day after. Movement matters. Fresh air matters.

## 4. Immerse Yourself in Music

With time and nowhere to rush, music becomes healing. Put on your headphones, lie down, and let yourself disappear into sound. Discover a new artist. Revisit a favorite album. Or stream an entire concert. Let music carry you.

## 5. Get Lost in Podcasts

Podcasts offer inspiration, insight, and ideas from people's real experiences. Whether you're interested in personal growth or professional development, there's a podcast for that. Explore local Estonian creators, or if you speak English or German, thousands more await. Pick your genre and listen. You'll be amazed how quickly the week passes.

## 6. Listen to an Audiobook

Beyond podcasts, audiobooks are a perfect recovery companion. Choose a story that calls to you. Settle in with headphones or a speaker. Let yourself drift into another world. Fiction, memoir, mystery—let the narrator carry you away.

## 7. Call People You Care About

Reach out to loved ones. Phone calls—real conversations, not texts—rebuild connection. Tell someone you're thinking of them. Listen to their news. This costs nothing and heals something real.

## The Bottom Line

Your recovery week is an investment in your vision's future. Treat it that way. Rest well, follow your surgeon's instructions, and trust the process. In seven days, you'll be back to life with clearer eyes and a calmer mind.
