## When Should You Have Laser Eye Surgery?

If you're wondering when to schedule [laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/), the honest answer is: there's no single perfect time. Every season has its advantages and drawbacks. That said, winter offers some genuinely compelling reasons to go ahead now.

## 1. Cooler Temperature and Lower Humidity

You may have heard the old saying about performing procedures only in months containing the letter 'r'. That wisdom belongs to a different era—modern surgical standards have evolved far beyond seasonal considerations. Summer is popular for eye procedures too. 

But here's what's true: cooler weather naturally reduces inflammation risk and may accelerate healing. Less humidity in the air means fewer irritants and a gentler environment for your eyes during recovery.

## 2. Darkness Works in Your Favour

For the first two weeks after [Flow3](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) or other laser procedures, some patients experience light sensitivity. Not everyone, but it happens.

While we typically lament winter's darkness, it's actually a blessing during recovery. You won't feel pressure to wear sunglasses constantly or worry about bright light outdoors. That darkness lets you move through daily life comfortably without squinting or discomfort.

## 3. Start the Year With a Real Win

Many of our patients list better vision as their New Year's resolution. Tackle it in January or February, and you're not just making a promise to yourself—you're actually keeping it within weeks. That's the kind of momentum that tends to spill over into other goals too.

## 4. Winter Encourages a Slower Pace

Longer daylight hours in spring and summer naturally energise us. We travel more, hike more, attend outdoor events. Winter is different—it's a season that naturally invites quieter living, time indoors, and gentle rest. That's exactly what recovery needs.

## 5. Spring Allergies Become Manageable

If spring allergies affect you, you know how miserable watery, itchy eyes can be with contact lenses. Laser eye surgery won't cure allergies, but clear vision makes getting through allergy season infinitely easier. You won't be juggling contacts and irritated eyes simultaneously.

## 6. Perfect Vision by Summer

Schedule your procedure in winter, and by summer your eyes will be fully healed. No contacts. No glasses. You'll wake up on a summer morning and see clearly without reaching for anything. Swimming, saunas, unexpected adventures—all clearer and easier.

Summer is packed with experiences worth seeing sharply: hiking trips, travels, spontaneous adventures, long evenings. Crisp vision makes them all richer.

## 7. No More Fogged Lenses

Winter itself brings a small daily frustration: glasses that fog up the moment you step indoors from the cold. One more reason winter's the perfect time to leave glasses behind for good.
