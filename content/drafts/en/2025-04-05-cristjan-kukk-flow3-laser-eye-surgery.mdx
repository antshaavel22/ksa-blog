## An Active Life, Finally Clear

Early summer brought Cristjan Kukk to KSA Silmakeskus for [Flow3 laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/). He's one of many Estonians who've spent half their lives abroad—in his case, England—and recently moved back home with his wife and son.

After finishing college and earning his CeMAP qualification in the UK, Cristjan built a career in finance and banking. He still works in that field today. But when the family grew, they made the decision to return to Estonia. For Cristjan, it's been the right call. He's genuinely happy to be back.

## Sport, Family, and Never Missing a Moment

Cristjan has many hobbies, but football is closest to his heart. He started playing for Keila FC as a boy in Estonia, then represented two school teams in England and trained at smaller Manchester clubs. Beyond the pitch, his whole family loves exploring Estonia and traveling. There are plans for plenty of trips ahead—they want to discover every corner of the country. Summers mean roller-skating, cycling, basketball, football, and whatever else looks fun.

What's interesting is that Cristjan has always been active. But since his laser procedure, his enthusiasm for hobbies and sport has actually grown. A few months ago, he started endurance training. One memory stands out: a warm summer day, cycling through the rain. Normally, glasses and rain don't mix. But without them for the first time, he could just keep riding through the downpour. That moment hit home—he realized how much he'd been missing.

## Life Through Glasses

Wearing glasses was routine and frustrating in equal measure. Fogged lenses ruined sports sessions and concerts. Without them, he couldn't function. Cristjan laughs remembering a trip years ago to Wembley Stadium with friends for an Estonia–England match. Everything was perfect—until he realized he'd left his glasses at home. In central London during rush hour, there's no chance to go back. It's like driving across the entire length of Estonia just to cross a few miles of the city. So he watched the whole match through a blur.

## Why He Chose Flow3

Moving back to Estonia sparked the decision. Being home meant dreaming of long summer days, midsummer celebrations, time by the sea, beautiful sunsets. Every beautiful moment made him want to experience it clearly, with his own eyes, without correction. Cristjan did his homework before visiting KSA. He had plenty of questions, but after the first consultation, there was no hesitation. The decision was made.

What sealed it? "The fact that the clinical team here all chose Flow3 for their own eyes," he says. "That tells you everything."
