Thanks to our long track record, our systems and processes are rock-solid and reliable. We deeply value our team's high level of competence and continuous professional development. Our optometrists, nurses, and customer service staff attend advanced training courses multiple times every year.

Flow3 isn't just a bladeless procedure—it's completely *touchless*. The eye is never touched, and no incision is made at all. This is only possible thanks to cutting-edge German technology used by the most respected and award-winning clinics worldwide.

One reason we're committed to Flow3's bladeless, touchless approach is the remarkably fast recovery time and ability to return to an active lifestyle. Just one week after the procedure, patients can do everything—contact sports, swimming, skydiving, you name it. There's no need to worry about your eyes, since late-stage complications are extremely rare with our bladeless, touchless method.
