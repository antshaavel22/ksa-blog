We've opened a new eye clinic in Tartu.

At the end of October, KSA Silmakeskus relocated its Tartu practice to a fresh, purpose-built space in Kvartal centre. We marked the occasion with an opening celebration — and it was clear that the Tartu community was ready for it.

Finding the right location took time. We needed a space that worked for our team and made sense for our patients. Kvartal centre, right in the heart of Tartu, ticked every box. The interior was designed by Lävi, an architecture studio that understands how clinical spaces should feel: modern, calm, welcoming.

The opening week brought together our partners, new faces, and familiar patients. Dr. Ants Haavel, our lead ophthalmologist, spoke about laser vision correction. We also heard from two of our patients — siblings Kristina and Johannes Sikk — who shared their experiences with [Flow3](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/), our flapless surface laser procedure. Throughout the day, visitors could explore the clinic and have their vision checked for nearsightedness.

We're grateful to everyone who came to celebrate with us. Your support means everything.

## What we offer in Tartu

At KSA Tartu, you'll find:

* Pre-procedure assessment for [Flow3 laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) — ideal if you wear glasses or contact lenses
* Comprehensive eye health audit for adults
* Eye examinations for children

## How to find us

We're on the 2nd floor of Kvartal centre.

**By car:** Enter from Riia Street via entrance 2, or from parking levels P1 and P2 via entrance 2. Stairs and lift available.

**Opening hours:** Monday–Thursday, 9:00–17:00
