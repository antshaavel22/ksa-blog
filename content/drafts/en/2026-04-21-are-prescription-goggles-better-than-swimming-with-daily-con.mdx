Prescription swimming goggles are significantly safer and better for your eye health than swimming with daily disposable contact lenses
. Eye care professionals and the FDA strictly advise against exposing contact lenses to any type of water, classifying swimming with contacts as an avoidable risk
.
The Advantages of Prescription Goggles Prescription goggles are considered the safest and most highly recommended solution for swimmers who need vision correction
. They feature lenses tailored exactly to your prescription while providing a watertight seal that keeps harmful bacteria, chlorine, and salt away from your eyes
. Because they completely prevent water from contacting the eyes, they eliminate the risk of water-related infections
. Furthermore, prescription goggles are a cost-effective, long-term investment that typically range between $20 and $40, and they will easily outlast the constant need to throw away daily disposable lenses
. Some prescription goggles and over-goggle masks also offer added benefits like UV protection and fog-resistant coatings
.
The Risks of Daily Disposables While wearing daily disposable contacts under tight-fitting swim goggles is sometimes suggested as a secondary option, it only reduces the risk of infection rather than eliminating it entirely
. Standard swim goggles do not completely prevent water leakage
. If even a small amount of non-sterile water seeps in, a soft contact lens can act like a sponge, absorbing water and trapping dangerous microbes directly against your highly sensitive cornea
. This significantly elevates the risk of contracting severe, sight-threatening eye infections, such as Acanthamoeba keratitis, which is notoriously difficult to treat and can result in permanent vision loss
.
If you do choose to swim with daily disposable lenses, it is absolutely mandatory to remove and discard them immediately after you finish swimming to minimize the chance of pathogens multiplying on the surface of your eye
. However, to enjoy the water with absolute safety, leaving your contacts in their case and relying on prescription goggles is the superior choice
