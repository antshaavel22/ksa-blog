## What Is Flow3?

[Flow3 is a flapless surface laser procedure](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) designed for myopia and astigmatism. It corrects combined refractive errors up to 9 diopters. The ideal age for any vision correction is 20–45 years old.

Flow3 is contact-free. No cut. No flap. Performed entirely with the Schwind Amaris 1050RS laser—one of the most advanced systems available. Recovery is natural and quick. Most patients return to normal activity within a week.
