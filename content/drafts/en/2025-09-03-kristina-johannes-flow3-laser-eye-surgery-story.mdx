By day, Kristina manages marketing at Sony Music Baltics. But that's only half the story.

She's a photographer, a fitness enthusiast, and an athlete across multiple disciplines. "I've done everything from dancing and football to athletics and gymnastics," she explains. "But in recent years, my brother got me into triathlon. I love running and cycling—sport is how I decompress and recharge from the stress of daily life."

Then there's the digital side. Kristina is also a content creator managing Instagram and TikTok. For someone juggling sports, photography, and content creation across multiple platforms, clear vision isn't a luxury—it's essential. Glasses were a complication she didn't need.

## Johannes' world: the pursuit of Olympic excellence

Johannes lives and breathes triathlon. At a young age, he's already compiled an impressive résumé: he's been crowned Estonian, Finnish, and Lithuanian triathlon champion. He placed 19th at the European U23 Championships and 28th at the World Student Championships.

His goal is clear: the Olympics.

This demands total commitment. "My day starts at 5:45 a.m.," Johannes describes his routine. "Swimming training begins at 6:30, then strength work, followed by cycling or running in the evening. Most days I train twice or three times." What drew him to triathlon? "The variety, for one—you never get bored dominating a single sport. Plus, I've always thrived in endurance athletics."

For an elite athlete, vision quality directly impacts performance. Every tenth of a second matters in competitive sport. Correcting vision without the vulnerability of flaps or stitches became the logical choice.

## Why Flow3 made sense for both

Kristina and Johannes arrived at the same procedure for different reasons, yet both recognised the same advantage: [Flow3](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) is flapless.

This matters. Traditional LASIK creates a corneal flap—a thin layer that's lifted and repositioned. For someone who swims, cycles, runs, and lives at the edge of physical extremes, that flap represents a vulnerability. Flow3 uses surface-level laser correction with no incision, no flap, faster healing. Recovery takes about a week rather than several.

For Kristina, it meant getting back to content creation, training, and shooting without weeks of caution. For Johannes, it meant returning to elite-level training without worry.

Both chose Flow3 because it aligned with their lifestyles—not because marketing promised something, but because the physics of the procedure solved a real problem.
