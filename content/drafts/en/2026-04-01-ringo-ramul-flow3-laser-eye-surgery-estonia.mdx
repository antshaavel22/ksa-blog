## From Glasses to Clear Vision: Ringo Ramul's Journey

Acclaimed Estonian actor and director Ringo Ramul made a life-changing decision on his 30th birthday: he chose to free himself from glasses through [Flow3 laser eye surgery](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) at KSA Silmakeskus. After years of thinking it over, he finally gave himself the gift of clear vision—and hasn't looked back.

Ringo is currently an actor at Ugala Theatre in Viljandi, where he's earned significant recognition, including colleagues' awards for Best Actor (2018 and 2021) and the audience award "Golden Apple" for Best Production (2020). He's widely regarded as one of Estonia's most talented theatre performers, known for his dedication and creative spark.

## Why He Waited So Long

Glasses had been woven into Ringo's identity for years. Even though he'd grown accustomed to them, his nearsightedness created awkward—and often hilarious—moments in everyday life.

"Without my glasses, the spa and sauna were comedy goldmines," he laughs. "I'd swim straight up to someone with a big smile, only to realise at the last second it was a complete stranger. In the sauna, I'd start entire conversations with the wrong person and wonder why my friend wasn't responding."

But the real catalyst came from those close to him. "Glasses felt like part of who I was, and I'd been thinking about surgery for ages. But friends and acquaintances who'd had the procedure at KSA kept praising it. Eventually, their encouragement tipped the balance. I decided it was time."

## The Procedure: Simpler Than Expected

What surprised Ringo most was how straightforward the experience turned out to be.

"Everything happened incredibly fast. I'd braced myself for something far more uncomfortable, but it was remarkably easy," he says. "The whole process felt smooth and professional. The team at KSA made me feel completely at ease."

This isn't unusual. [Flow3 is a flapless surface laser procedure](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/)—there's no cut, no flap to create. Recovery typically takes around a week, and many patients notice improved vision within days. It's why the entire clinical team at KSA chose Flow3 for their own eyes. When the surgeons trust a procedure enough to use it themselves, that tells you something.

## Life After: Freedom in the Everyday

For someone in theatre—where presence and physicality matter—clear vision without barriers has been transformative. Ringo now enjoys life's simple moments without the friction of glasses. No more awkward spa encounters. No more squinting to recognise a friend across a room. Just clarity.

"At 30, I gave myself the gift of excellent vision," he reflects. "It's one of the best decisions I've made."
