## We've opened a new clinic in Tartu

Late October brought a major milestone — we relocated KSA Tartu clinic to a new home, and we celebrated in style.

Finding the right space in the heart of Tartu wasn't rushed. We wanted somewhere that matched our standards and our patients' expectations. We found it at Kvartali, and interior design studio Lävi created something warm and welcoming.

Our opening event filled the new clinic with partners, familiar faces, and new patients. Dr Ants Haavel took the stage to discuss [laser eye surgery procedures](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/), and we heard a personal story from Kristina and Johannes Sikk — two of our patients who'd undergone treatment. We watched a film featuring their experience, answered questions, and gave visitors a tour of the space. People also had their vision checked on the day.

We're genuinely grateful to everyone who came to meet us during opening week. It meant a lot.

## Services at KSA Tartu

*   Pre-procedure [Flow3 eye examination](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) for myopia patients and contact lens wearers
*   Comprehensive eye examination (Audit) for adults
*   Vision screening for children

## Finding us

Our new clinic is on the 2nd floor of Kvartali:

*   From Riia Street: Use entrance 2 (stairs or lift)
*   From car park levels P1 and P2: Use entrance 2 (stairs or lift)

**Opening hours:** Monday–Friday, 9:00–17:00

Questions about [Flow3](https://ksa.ee/en/laser-eye-surgery/) or [ICB lens replacement](https://ksa.ee/en/icb-time)? Our Tartu team is ready to help.
