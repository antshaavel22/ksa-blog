## We're Inviting Our Kitesurfing Clients to Pärnu

Join us **4–6 July** in Pärnu for the Kite Challenge series. Twenty of our kitesurfing clients have secured something special: exclusive access to a professional freestyle masterclass with **Kristiin Oja**, Estonia's world-ranked freestyle kitesurfer. 

And here's the best part — it's completely free.

You'll get:
- Hands-on coaching from a professional
- VIP zone access (restricted to KSA participants only)
- Full meals throughout the event
- Entry to the evening afterparty

Kristiin Oja isn't just any kitesurfer. She's ranked **7th in the world** in freestyle kitesurfing and knows what it takes to push the sport to the next level. Now she's ready to share her tricks and experience with our community.

## What You Need to Know

**This masterclass is for experienced kitesurfers.** If you're already comfortable on the water and know your way around freestyle tricks, you'll get the most from Kristiin's coaching. Absolute beginners may find the session challenging, as it focuses on advancing technique rather than learning basics.

Spots fill fast — only **20 exclusive wristbands** are available. These grant entry to the VIP area and everything it includes.

## The Bigger Picture

KSA Silmakeskus backs ambitious people with big goals. Kristiin embodies that — and so do many of our patients. We believe clear vision matters, whether you're chasing world rankings or simply living life to the fullest. That's why we support events like this.

The Aloha Kite Challenge runs across **4–6 July** on Pärnu Beach, featuring competitive kite and twintip events, plus entertainment and demos throughout the day. The evening finale is an **afterparty** headlined by Vaiko Eplik & Eliit, Angus, Daniel Levi, and more.
